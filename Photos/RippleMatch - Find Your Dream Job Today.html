NIELSEN CONNECT INVESTS IN YOU!

SET YOURSELF UP FOR GROWTH, IMPACT, AND LEADERSHIP IN OUR EARLY CAREER PROGRAMS 


Who we are: 

Nielsen Connect has explored what’s important to shoppers for more than 90 years, and businesses continue to rely on our data and insights. With groundbreaking technology, we provide a truly complete picture of the complex and changing marketplace, brick and mortar as well as e-commerce, that manufacturers and retailers need to innovate and grow their businesses.  


At Nielsen Connect, we invest in you. Nielsen Connect’s Early Career Program (ECP) offers a powerful and unique experience for top undergraduates interested in a career with the world’s leading provider of global marketing information and consumer insights. The Early Career Program (ECP) is a challenging 24-month rotational program designed to develop leaders who will position Nielsen Connect for growth while at the same time investing heavily in your growth holistically to set you up for success. The assignments provide challenging on-the-job learning and development opportunities in Chicago. Opportunities are currently available beginning July 2021 in two tracks:


OPERATIONS 

The Operations track develops future Nielsen Connect leaders who can provide clients with the innovative data management and technology solutions they need to make critical decisions. Associates take on roles of increasing responsibility during four six-month hands-on rotational assignments over two years.  

Project Examples in Operations include:

  • Understanding Metrics That Drive Business Value – Establish key metrics to be used in determining acceptable performance levels of a business process.
  • Deepen Analytical Skills – Analyze performance and delivery data for global departments to identify root causes and develop improvement focused action plans.
  • Challenging the Status Quo – Identification of process issues impacting data quality and Nielsen employee efficiency and implementation of solutions to correct these issues.
  • Program Management - Rolling out new products or features as part of large scale changes to a line of business with cross functional exposure or leading a team.

Applicable Majors: 

B.S. in Industrial/Systems Engineering, Supply Chain and Operations Management, Business Information Technology, Mechanical Engineering, BBA Operations & Technology Management, Industrial Management with a concentration in Operations 


DATA SCIENCE 

You’ll work with Nielsen Connect’s Data Science team on data analysis, data management and creative technology solutions during four six-month hands-on rotational assignments over two years. 

Project Examples in Data Science: 

  • Global Connect Non-Cooperator Estimation: Utilizing python and machine learning use our consumer panel to estimate sales of store level point-of-sale data, enabling our retail clients to predict store sales.
  • Using Bayesian Inference to combine data sources: This project uses Bayesian inference methodologies to develop a proof of principle for how to combine different datasets together. Technologies include Python, PyMC3, Pyro, and matplotlib.
  • Machine Learning Ensembling Methods For Item Coding: Using advanced machine learning techniques to leverage our vast amounts of unlabeled data and work toward full automation of ingesting and manipulating panelist data into a form usable by our clients.

Applicable Majors: 

B.S. in Statistics, Math, Economics, Market Research, Research Methodology, Data Science, Quantitative Social Science, Computer Science majors with a focus in math or statistics will be considered 


Selection Criteria:

  • Significant leadership experience in college extra-curricular activities, community involvement or previous employment
  • Minimum cumulative overall GPA of 3.0
  • Less than two years full-time work experience
  • Willingness to relocate to Chicago, IL if necessary
  • Permanent US work authorization (required)


 NOTE: While our teams are currently working remotely, once we are safely back to an in office work environment, these positions will be onsite in our Chicago office.


ABOUT NIELSEN


Arthur C. Nielsen, who founded Nielsen in 1923, is the original name in consumer intelligence. After decades of helping companies look to the future, we are setting the foundation for our future by becoming NielsenIQ. We continue to be the undisputed industry leaders as evidenced by our experience and unmatched integrity. As we move forward, we are focused on providing the best retail and consumer data platform, enabling better innovation, faster delivery, and bolder decision-making. We are unwavering in our commitment to these ideals and passionate about helping clients achieve success.
